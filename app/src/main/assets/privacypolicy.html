Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect certain information from our patients (consultees) in order to provide consulting services and improve the patient experience. The information we collect may include:

  • Full Name: We require your full name for identification and consultation purposes.
  • Contact Information: We collect your contact information, such as phone number and email address, to communicate with you regarding appointments and health-related information.
  • Address: We may collect your address to provide personalized recommendations or facilitate home-based consultations.
  • Medical History: We securely collect and process your medical history to provide personalized and effective consulting services.
  • Preferences: We may collect information about your preferences, such as preferred consultation timings or specific health concerns, to offer a personalized experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing Consulting Services: We use your information to schedule appointments, offer consulting services, and provide medical advice.
  • Communicating with You: We may use your contact information to send you appointment reminders, health-related updates, and important information regarding your consultations.
  • Medical Record Keeping: We securely process your medical history to ensure accurate and informed consultations.
  • Improving Our Services: We analyze patient data to enhance our consulting services and improve the overall patient experience.

Sharing Your Information

We understand the importance of your privacy and do not disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you provide explicit consent for us to do so.
  • Legal Obligations: We may share your information if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • Healthcare Providers: We may share relevant information with trusted healthcare providers to ensure comprehensive and coordinated care.

Security Measures

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented reasonable measures to protect it. We employ industry-standard security practices to safeguard your data, including:

  • Secure Storage: Your personal and medical information is stored securely in encrypted databases.
  • Data Encryption: We use encryption technology to protect sensitive information, such as medical history and contact details.
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our systems and infrastructure to ensure the highest level of security.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates or modifications will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically. Your continued use of our consulting services after any changes to the privacy policy signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.

Your Choices and Rights

You have certain rights and choices regarding the personal information we collect:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information by contacting us directly. We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate your request, subject to any legal or contractual obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You have the option to opt-out of receiving health-related updates or communications from us. You can do this by contacting us.
  • Data Retention: We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.
